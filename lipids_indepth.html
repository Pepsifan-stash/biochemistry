Fat vs. Sugar

Takeaway: Fats provide 9 kcal/g compared to 4 kcal/g in sugars because carbon atoms in fatty acids are more reduced (carrying more electrons to donate to oxygen).

Saturation

Takeaway: Double bonds in natural fatty acids are usually in the "cis" configuration, creating a 30° kink that prevents tight packing and lowers the melting point.

Trans Fats

Takeaway: Trans fatty acids are produced by industrial hydrogenation; they mimic the linear shape of saturated fats, raising LDL (bad) cholesterol levels.
